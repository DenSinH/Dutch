program:
    |compound statement*
    |id GOES ?(FROM int expr) TO int expr END compound statement...
    |WHILE condition DO END(:)

compound statement:
    |statement* END

statement:
    |assignment     id (OBJ ASSIGN|STR ASSIGN) expr
    |PRINT expr

OP:
    |ADD|SUB|MUL|DIV|OR|AND

expr:
    |NOT? (id|value) (OP NOT? (id|value))*
